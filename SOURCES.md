# Free SFX Sources for 《诡街》

**Pixabay (CC0, recommended)**: https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/
- UI: search "ui" or "click"
- Child laugh: "children laughing"
- Creak: "creaking" or "wood creak"
- Phone ring: "phone ring"
- Butcher: "butcher" or "chopping"
- Birds, cat, bicycle bell, construction: direct search

**Mixkit (free)**: https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- Clicks, old phone ring, switches

**Other**:
- Paper rustle/panel: Search "paper rustling" Pixabay
- Old elevator/creak: Soundsnap or ElevenLabs free sounds
- General: Freesound.org (filter CC0)

**Note for Chinese specific (market hawking, old radio)**: Low match in free Western libs. Use Suno generation instead for authenticity and quality.
